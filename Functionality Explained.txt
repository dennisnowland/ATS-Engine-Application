? ATS ENGINE — HighLevel Summary of All Rules (Human Tone)
“How your system thinks, scores, routes, and automates — in plain English.”
1. The Compare Rule (Your Core Scoring Brain)
This is the rule that decides how well a job matches you.
It looks at things like:
* Your skills
* Your seniority
* Your domain experience
* Your Agile background
* Your engineering background
* Your clearance
* Your remote/hybrid preferences
Then it gives the job a Fit Score out of 100.
How to use it
Paste a job description ? the engine reads it ? scores it ? tells you how strong the match is.
If the score is:
* 93% or higher ? goes to the Main Table
* Below 93% ? goes to the Under93 Table
* Hard fail (wrong domain, wrong seniority, wrong location) ? Under93
This rule is the foundation of everything else.
2. Seniority Mismatch Rule
This rule checks whether the job is:
* Too junior
* Too senior
* A leadership role
* A handson coding role
* A hybrid role
It prevents you from being matched to jobs that don’t fit your level.
How to use it
You don’t need to do anything — it runs automatically. It protects you from wasting time on roles that don’t align with your experience.
3. Domain Match Rule
This checks whether the job is in a domain you’re strong in:
* Software
* Cloud
* Data
* Government
* Financial services
* Agile delivery
* Engineering leadership
It penalises roles in:
* Hardware
* Electronics
* Mechanical engineering
* Embedded systems
How to use it
Again, automatic. It ensures you stay in your strongest domains.
4. Mandatory Requirement Rule
If a job says:
* “Must have X”
* “Essential”
* “Mandatory”
…and you don’t have it ? the score is capped at 50%.
How to use it
If a job fails this rule, you can safely ignore it — it’s not worth applying.
5. Disqualifier Rule
Instant fail for things like:
* DV clearance
* 5 days onsite
* NonUK location
* Hardware engineering degree required
How to use it
This rule saves you time by filtering out impossible roles.
6. Travel Feasibility Rule
If the job requires:
* 3+ days onsite outside Scotland ? fail
* 1–2 days ? small penalty
How to use it
This keeps your job search realistic and aligned with your lifestyle.
7. Salary Alignment Rule
If the salary is too low, the score drops. If it’s high, the score stays strong.
How to use it
You don’t need to do anything — it helps you avoid underpaid roles.
8. Table Routing Rule
After scoring, the job is automatically placed in:
Main Table
* Fit ? 93%
* Interviewing roles
* Autocreated interview entries
Under93 Table
* Fit < 93%
* Hard fails
* Seniority mismatches
* Domain mismatches
How to use it
You simply check the right table depending on your priority.
9. CV Selection Rule
The engine automatically chooses the best CV for the job:
* C# CV
* PHP CV
* Scrum Master CV
* Agile Coach CV
* Delivery Manager CV
* TPM CV
* PMO CV
* Engineering Manager CV
How to use it
You don’t need to pick a CV — the system does it for you.
10. Notes Engine
This rule writes a short explanation for each job:
* Why it scored high
* Why it scored low
* What mismatches exist
* What strengths you have
* Which CV was chosen
How to use it
Read the notes to understand the match at a glance.
11. Status Engine
Automatically updates job status:
* New ? To Review
* Good match ? High Priority
* Submitted ? Submitted
* Rejected ? Rejected
* Interview detected ? Interviewing
How to use it
You don’t need to manually track statuses — the engine does it.
12. AutoCoverLetter Rule
If Fit ? 93% ? the system generates a tailored cover letter.
How to use it
Just paste the job — the cover letter appears automatically.
13. AutoInterviewPrep Rule
If Fit ? 93% ? the system adds:
“Full interviewprep pack generated…”
And you can request the full pack on demand.
How to use it
Ask: “Generate full prep pack for ID X”
14. Duplicate Detection Rule
If you paste the same job twice:
* It increments the duplicate count
* It does NOT create a new row
How to use it
You don’t need to worry about duplicates — the engine handles them.
15. Missing Interview Entry Rule
If you get an interview email for a job that isn’t in the table:
* The system creates a new row
* Sets Fit = 93%
* Sets Status = Interviewing
How to use it
Just forward or paste the interview email — the system fills the gap.
